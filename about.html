About Gradify

Gradify is a powerful tool designed to streamline the testing process for educational institutions. It features automated test case execution, test plan creation, and defect tracking. Gradify is user-friendly, customizable, and complies with industry standards for data protection. With Gradify, educational institutions can save time, improve collaboration and communication, and provide a better assessment experience for their students.

Our Team

Our team is comprised of experienced developers who have a passion for creating innovative tools and solutions that make the testing process more efficient and effective. With customizable test plans, advanced result analysis, and secure data storage, Gradify is the perfect solution for modern educational institutions seeking to improve their test procedures.

Our Mission

At Gradify, our mission is to make test management easy for educational institutes. We offer a secure and customizable platform that streamlines the entire testing process, from test creation to result analysis. With Gradify, institutes can save time and resources while providing a better learning experience for their students.
